Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a grying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has meted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center ever 20 seconds(as if yo are making an omeletee)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
